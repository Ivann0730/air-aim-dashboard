# AIR AIM — Airport Intelligent Response & AI Monitoring  
Real-time Passenger Flow Analytics • Automated Queue Detection • Predictive Congestion Forecasting  

AIR AIM is an AI-powered monitoring platform that transforms airport CCTV feeds into actionable operational intelligence.  
It helps airports shift from **reactive congestion management** to **proactive, data-driven decision making.**

---

## ✨ Features

- **YOLOv8 Person Detection**  
  Tracks passengers in real time across multiple CCTV feeds.

- **ROI-Based Analytics**  
  Separate monitoring zones such as Check-in, Central Walkway, and Security.

- **Automated Queue Detection**  
  Identifies clustering and detects early queue formation.

- **Density Heatmaps**  
  Visualize passenger concentration and movement patterns.

- **Crowd Level Classification**  
  Automatically labels areas as Normal, Busy, or Crowded.

- **Time-Series Forecasting**  
  Uses Moving Average, ARIMA, or Prophet to predict future congestion levels.

- **Multi-Source Input Support**  
  Webcam, uploaded videos, and RTSP/IP camera streams.

- **CSV Logging**  
  Saves people counts, queue alerts, ROI stats, and timestamps.
